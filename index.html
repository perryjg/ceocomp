How many lifetimes would it take you to earn just one year's salary for one of the top Atlanta CEOs?

Assuming you start working when you turn 21 and stop just before your 65th birthday, we calculate how many working lifetimes it would take to earn the equivalent of just one year's take-home pay for the chief executive officers of Atlanta's top publically-traded companies.

CEO take-home pay, or their "realizable pay," includes the value of all the cash, perks and company stock taht a CEO gains ownership of during a year.

The median family income in the Atlanta metro is $50,000 a year. That would result in take-home pay of about $38,780. Minimum wage is $7.35 an hour, or a little less than $15,288 annually. Estimated take-home pay would be $6.40 an hour or $13,300 annually.
